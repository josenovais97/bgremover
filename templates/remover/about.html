Our mission

Most online image tools upload your photos to a server, put them behind a paywall, or stamp them with a watermark. ClearBG takes the opposite approach: the AI and all processing run on your device, so your images never leave your browser. That makes every tool genuinely free, unlimited, private by design, and fast.
