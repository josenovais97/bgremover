How to make a passport photo

1.Drop a portrait

Use a front-facing photo with a neutral expression and even lighting. We remove the background automatically.

2.Pick a size

Choose your country and align your head between the guide lines — crown near the top line, chin near the bottom.

3.Download or print

Save the exact pixel size at 300 DPI, or a 6×4" sheet of copies to print at any pharmacy or photo kiosk.

Supported sizes

  • US — 2×2 in (600×600 px) — passport, visa, green card
  • EU / Schengen / UK — 35×45 mm
  • India / Australia — 35×45 mm
  • Canada — 50×70 mm
  • China visa — 33×48 mm
  • Custom — any size in millimetres

The guides help you meet the head-size and position rules most authorities use. Always check your government's exact requirements before submitting — this is a free helper tool, not an official service.

Everything runs in your browser, so your photo — a sensitive personal document — never leaves your device.
